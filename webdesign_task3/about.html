About Us

The Malta College of Arts, Science & Technology (MCAST) is the country's leading vocational education and training institution. Our mission is to provide quality education that is both practical and career-focused. We support our students in developing the skills and confidence they need to succeed in today's competitive job market.

MCAST offers a wide range of full-time and part-time courses from certificates to doctoral degrees. Our teaching is hands-on and industry-oriented, ensuring students get real-world experience while studying.

We believe in innovation, inclusion, and collaboration, striving to make a positive impact in our community and beyond.
